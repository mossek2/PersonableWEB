What is Personable

Say hello to the future

Personable is a simple application that allows you to connect with those around you in a real and authentic way. The mobile application that gives you the power to seamlessly exchange contact information in any social setting. Gone are the days of forgotten names, numbers on napkins and reciting email addresses.

Super Social

Just for you!

How would you like to communicate? Personable is customised specifically for you and supports all your favourite social networks and messaging services.

Whether you’re a Tweeter, Tumbler or WhatsApper you can skip the username exchange and let Personable handle the details.

Our Super Simple Setup
